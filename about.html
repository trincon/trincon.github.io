About me

I am a rising senior at the University of Florida pursuing a Computer Science major and a Digital Arts and Sciences minor. I have long been drawn to the arts with a focus on 2D mediums: drawing and painting. As such, this silent need for expression manifested itself into a desire to create professionally, hence the Digital Arts Minor. This has led to an interest in photography and photoediting software like Adobe Photoshop and Lightroom, where I can play with color and lighting to tell a story. In addition, I use music as a mode of expression by toying with the guitar and piano and by singing along to those with talent.

In my free time, I am often exploring the beauty world: researching new fashion trends, reading up on new skin care regimens, and envying those with the artistic skills to create elaborate makeup looks. I also love to travel and have been blessed with the opportunities to visit countries like Spain, Morocco, Portugal, the Netherlands, Denmark, England, and Sweden. Hopefully, that list will never grow static.

By the time I'm old and grey, I hope to have authored at least one book and acquired at least one new language, preferably French.
